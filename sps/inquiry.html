/ Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Do you take International clients?

    +

    Yes — in fact — our client base is not segmented, we take projects from all over the world. Although we are based in Southeast Asia, we take various sizes of businesses from various part of the world.

  2. What is your pricing structure?

    +

    Depending on the kind of services, we have various pricing structure. For web & app development for example, we charge on an hourly basis. For other services like strategy and media production, we calculate the effort at the beginning and use a per-project basis pricing structure.

  3. Do you always have a team available for my project?

    +

    No, we don't always have a team ready. We are limited in team size and we only do only a few projects at a time to ensure maximum quality and deliverables.

  4. What is the general process of doing project with Fleava?

    +

    Generally, we start with collecting brief and project requirements. From there, we gather and analize all the data, and generate estimates based on all the brief. Then we proceed with the paperwork before we start executing the project, phase by phase.

  5. How do you communicate with your clients?

    +

    Generally we keep all project communications on Slack. We create a Slack channel for every project we do.

    For project management, we use Jira Software by Attlassian. That's where you will see all progress, technical details and timelines, while for online meetings we can communicate through any platforms such as Zoom or Google Meet.

  6. Why Fleava?

    +

    We are a world-class team of industry-leading specialists who share a common goal — to shape the digital landscape of tomorrow.

    We prioritize measurable results and has assisted businesses in developing sustainable growth to generate momentum that lasts for years — and that's why we are trusted by Global Brands worldwide.
